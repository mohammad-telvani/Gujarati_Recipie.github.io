    Ingredients

  • 1 cup soya flour
  • 1 cup chickpea flour
  • 1/4 cup rice flour
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper powder
  • 1/3 cup water

    Instruction

  1. In a large bowl, combine the soya flour, chickpea flour, rice flour, red chili powder, coriander powder, turmeric powder, cumin powder, salt, black pepper powder, carom seeds, and baking soda.
  2. Add the oil and mix well.
  3. Gradually add the water and knead to a soft dough.
  4. Meanwhile, make the masala by combining the fennel seeds, black pepper, red chili powder, black salt, salt, mango powder, citric acid, garlic powder, and sugar in a small bowl.
  5. After 15 minutes, divide the dough into small balls.
  6. Roll out each ball into a thin log.
  7. Cut the log into sticks about 2 inches long.
  8. Heat some oil in a frying pan over medium heat.
  9. Drain the fried sticks on paper towels.
  10. Serve hot or cold.
