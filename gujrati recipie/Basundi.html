    Ingredients

  • 1 liter full-fat milk
  • 1/2 cup sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 1/4 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • A few strands of saffron (soaked in warm milk)
  • Chopped nuts (almonds, pistachios, cashews) for garnishing
  • Raisins for garnishing (optional)

    Instruction

  1. Boil the Milk
  2. Simmer the Milk
  3. Add Sugar
  4. Add Flavorings
  5. Garnish
  6. Chill and Serve
  7. Storage
