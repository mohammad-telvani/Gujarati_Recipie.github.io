    Ingredients

  • 1 cup mustard seeds
  • 1/2 cup fenugreek seeds
  • 1/4 cup fennel seeds
  • 1/4 cup cumin seeds
  • 1/4 cup coriander seeds
  • 1/4 cup nigella seeds (kalonji)
  • 15-20 dried red chili peppers (adjust to taste)
  • 1 tablespoon black salt
  • 1 tablespoon asafoetida (hing)
  • 1 tablespoon turmeric powder
  • 1 cup mustard oil (for roasting the spices)

    Instruction

  1. Wash the raw mangoes thoroughly and pat them dry.
  2. Make two perpendicular slits in each mango, forming a cross, but do not cut them into separate pieces. Remove the seeds if they come out while making the slits.
  3. Mix the salt and turmeric powder in a bowl. Rub this mixture all over the mangoes, making sure they are well-coated. Place the mangoes in a clean, dry glass or ceramic jar, and cover the jar loosely with a clean cloth. Leave the mangoes to dry in the sun for 2-3 days, turning them occasionally.
  4. Allow the roasted spices to cool. Then, grind them to a fine powder using a spice grinder or mortar and pestle.
  5. Heat the mustard oil in a pan until it reaches its smoking point. Turn off the heat and let it cool slightly.
  6. Add the asafoetida and turmeric powder to the cooled oil. Mix well.
  7. Pour this spiced oil mixture over the ground spice mixture and mix thoroughly. The mixture should resemble a thick paste.
  8. Once the mangoes are dry, carefully stuff the spice mixture into the slits of each mango, using a small spoon or your fingers. Press gently to pack the stuffing.
  9. Place the stuffed mangoes back into the jar, ensuring they are tightly packed.
  10. Pour the remaining mustard oil over the mangoes, ensuring they are fully submerged. You may need more oil if the mangoes are not completely covered.
  11. Seal the jar tightly with a lid.
  12. Place the jar in a sunny spot for about a week, allowing the flavors to develop. Shake the jar gently every day to distribute the spices and oil.
  13. After a week, the Stuffed Mango Achar will be ready to eat. You can store it at room temperature or in the refrigerator, depending on your preference.
